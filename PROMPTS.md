# Prompt 1

Generate a personalized 2-day travel itinerary for Rishikesh under ₹10000 for adventure lovers.

### Example Input

Destination: Rishikesh
Days: 2
Budget: ₹10000
Interests: Adventure, Nature

### Example Output

Provide a structured itinerary with:

- Day-wise schedule
- Places to visit
- Food recommendations
- Estimated budget
- Travel tips
